<objective>
Extend `renderMarkdown()` in `markdownRenderer.ts` to inject slugified `id` attributes into every heading (`h1`–`h6`) in the rendered HTML. Export `slugify` and `extractRawText` as pure functions for direct unit testing. The IDs produced here are the stable identifiers used by OutlinePane (Plan 03) to scroll headings into view via `document.getElementById(id)`.

Purpose: Provides the heading → DOM ID mapping that is the foundation for both click-to-scroll (OUTLINE-02) and active section tracking (OUTLINE-03). Without stable heading IDs, OutlinePane cannot locate its scroll targets.
Output: Extended `markdownRenderer.ts` with exported slug utilities and a heading renderer; expanded `markdownRenderer.test.ts` with TDD coverage of slug logic and id injection.
</objective>
